Full Stack Developer

Windward is the world’s pioneer in building marine vertical AI technology. We live and breath shipping and data. We partner with the world’s most impactful enterprises and governments  and empower them to tackle their hardest maritime challenges.
Backed by some of the world’s leading technology, policy and business leaders as well as top-tier institutional investors we leverage on our data platform and build cutting edge AI technology to predict safety and security risk and make an impact in the real world . It’s an exciting and tough journey, and we are looking for a Full stack developer to join our team.

As a Full Stack Developer at Windward you will use cutting-edge technologies, including angular.js and node.js, to build a beautiful and intelligent UI for handling maritime big data. Working closely with product managers and designers, you will be part of a tight-knit, dynamic, hands-on team that takes pride in its exceptional coding standards and development practices. As part of our team, your key challenge will be making massive amounts of data accessible, ensuring that the user experience is smooth and intuitive.

Requirements:

  • 3+ years of Full Stack development experience
  • Experience integrating with APIs
  • Experience in Angular.js
  • Experience with the latest features of the Javascript language
  • Experience in node.js packages including gulp, express, pug, babel
